📘 ✅ IF YOU MISS 4–5 DAYS — WHAT TO DO
✅ RULE 1 — Do NOT Restart Module From Beginning
After gap:
❌ Don’t restart whole module
❌ Don’t rewatch all videos
❌ Don’t panic


You are revising — not restarting.
✅ RULE 2 — First Day Back = Warm-Up Day
On comeback day:
Do only this:
Solve 2 old problems:
1 Array problem
1 Current-module problem (Recursion)
Without videos first.
This wakes up your brain patterns again.
Time: ~45–60 min only.

✅ RULE 3 — Second Day Back = Light Resume
Day 2 after gap:
Watch last learned topic video again (only last one)
Solve 1–2 problems from that topic
Do 1 array revision problem
Normal pace — not overload.

✅ RULE 4 — Third Day Back = Normal Plan
From Day 3:
Return to normal schedule:
New module → 1–2 problems
Old revision → 1 problem
Timer method
Notes method
